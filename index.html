About Us

CodeBlue is an innovative Third Party Administrator that specializes in insurance property claims. We use proven science and revolutionary technology to reduce cycle time, decrease severity, and improve the policyholder experience, while providing insurers a more predictable claim outcome.

CodeBlue utilizes a customizable Virtual Claims Strategy that allows adjusters to evaluate and manage claims from their desktop or mobile device, eliminating the need to travel to each loss. That results in a quicker resolution for every claim and a happier policyholder.
